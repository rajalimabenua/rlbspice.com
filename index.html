Indonesia's Trusted Spice Exporter — PT Raja Lima Benua (RLB Spice)

PT Raja Lima Benua, operating as RLB Spice at rlbspice.com, is a professional Indonesian spice supplier and exporter based in Surabaya, East Java, Indonesia. We specialise in bulk export of premium Indonesian spices including turmeric (kunyit), clove (cengkeh), nutmeg (pala), ginger (jahe), galangal (lengkuas), sand ginger (kencur), Lampung black pepper (lada hitam), Bangka white pepper (lada putih Muntok), Indonesian cassia cinnamon (kayu manis), and coriander seed (ketumbar).

We serve importers, food manufacturers, distributors, and specialty spice buyers across Europe, Asia, the Middle East, Australia, and the Americas. Our spices are sourced farm-direct, export-certified, and available in bulk and custom retail packaging.
